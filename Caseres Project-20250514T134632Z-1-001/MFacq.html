Frequently Asked Questions

Admission Requirements

Is the program recognized by the CHED?

Yes, the Criminology program is duly recognized and accredited by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED).

Southern Mindanao Institute of Technology (SMIT) welcomes aspiring criminology students who possess the passion and dedication to serve in the field of criminal justice. To apply for the Bachelor of Science in Criminology program, prospective students must prepare and submit the following documents.

Can students take licensure exams after graduation?

Yes, graduates are eligible to take the Criminologist Licensure Examination administered by the PRC.

General Requirements

Completed Application Form

Applicants must duly accomplish the official application form provided by the admissions office.

High School Diploma or Equivalent

A certified copy of the high school diploma or its recognized equivalent is required to verify educational attainment.

Certificate of Good Moral Character

This document, usually issued by the school principal or guidance counselor, attests to the applicantâ€™s good conduct and ethical behavior.

Birth Certificate (PSA/NSO Issued)

A photocopy of the birth certificate issued by the Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) or National Statistics Office (NSO) is needed for personal identification.

Recent 2x2 ID Photographs

Applicants must provide recent, passport-style 2x2 ID photos with a white background.
